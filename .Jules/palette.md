## 2024-05-19 - Improve Form Accessibility and Loading States
**Learning:** In Angular templates, dynamically rendered elements via `*ngIf` within a container without an `aria-live` attribute are not always reliably announced by screen readers when they appear. Additionally, not disabling a submit button during asynchronous operations can lead to duplicate form submissions, impacting both UX and backend data integrity.
**Action:** Consistently apply `aria-live="polite"` to persistent wrapper containers rather than directly on conditionally rendered content to guarantee reliable screen reader announcements. For async form submissions, always bind the submit button's `disabled` state to the loading variable and provide clear text feedback (e.g., "Processing...") to prevent duplicate submissions and enhance user feedback.
